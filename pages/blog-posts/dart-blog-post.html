Dart V2.12 with Null Safety!!!

What a milestone! The Dart team finally managed to bring null safety to this language.

Believe it or not, but Dart is only the third language, that provides such a feature by default!

Apples Swift and JetBrains Kotlin represent the other two languages with such a feature.

From now on, your IDE will support you to prevent such a mistake and check if a variable could be null and will address that problem to you!
